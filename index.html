HackIR (RidgeHacks) is an annual hackathon hosted by Iroquois Ridge High School. A hackathon is a live-event where participants, "hackers", spend the course of 12 hours creating, developing, designing, and pitching a software or hardware project they build from scratch. HackIR has been apart of the MLH Local Hack Day for 2 years and this year is no exception! Local Hack Day is a global hackathon uniting thousands of students across the world. Participants across 100+ distributed campuses are able to connect with their local communities and other hackers through digital workshops, social media, and a global livestream.
